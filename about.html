iCoder

iCoder was launched in October 2014 and incorporated as iCoder, Inc. The founder is a software developer who took up programming after graduate school and created iCoder as a way to streamline a student's progress from beginner to being job-ready. iCoder is a non-profit organization that consists of an interactive learning web platform, an online community forum, chat rooms, online publications and local organizations that intend to make learning software development accessible to anyone.
